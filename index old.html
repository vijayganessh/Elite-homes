Terms & Conditions

Please read these terms carefully before commencing any construction project with Elite Homes.

📋 1. Quotation & Agreement

  • All quotations are valid for 30 days from the date of issue.
  • A formal agreement must be signed before commencement of any work.
  • The quoted rate is based on the selected package specifications. Any deviation from the standard specifications will attract additional costs.
  • Rates are subject to change based on prevailing market prices of materials at the time of procurement.

💰 2. Payment Schedule

  • Advance: 15% before commencement
  • Basement Level: 10%
  • Ground Floor Roof Level: 15%
  • First Floor Roof Level: 20%
  • Second Floor Roof Level: 20%
  • After Flooring: 15%
  • Balance: After completion
  • Extra Work Payments: To be made as and when completed

🏗️ 3. Scope of Work

  • The scope covers structural work, brickwork, plastering, flooring, electrical, plumbing, painting, and door/window installation as per the chosen package.
  • The following are NOT included unless specifically agreed: compound wall, landscaping, borewell, overhead water tank, modular kitchen, false ceiling, and interior decorative work.
  • All work will be carried out as per the approved structural drawings and local municipal bye-laws.

📦 4. Materials

  • All materials will be as per the specifications listed in the chosen package. Substitutions will only be made with client approval.
  • The client has the right to inspect materials before use on site.
  • If the client opts for materials of higher grade, the cost difference will be borne by the client.
  • Sand availability and type (M-Sand / P-Sand) may vary based on local supply.

📅 5. Project Timeline

  • The project timeline will be agreed upon at the time of signing the contract.
  • Delays caused by client-side decisions (material upgrades, design changes, delayed payments) will not be counted in the contractor's timeline.
  • Force majeure events (floods, government restrictions, material scarcity) may extend the timeline without penalty.
  • A detailed project schedule will be provided within 7 days of commencement.

🛡️ 6. Warranty & Defect Liability

  • A 12-month defect liability period is provided from the date of possession for structural defects.
  • Plumbing and electrical issues arising from workmanship (not wear and tear) are covered for 6 months.
  • Warranty does not cover damage caused by misuse, natural disasters, or modifications made by the client after possession.

📜 7. Permits & Approvals

  • The client is responsible for obtaining all necessary building plan approvals from the local authority (CMDA / DTCP / Panchayat).
  • The contractor will assist in preparing required documentation for plan approval upon request (additional charges apply).
  • Any compounding fees or penalties due to deviations from the approved plan that are client-directed will be borne by the client.

⚖️ 8. Dispute Resolution

  • Any disputes arising from this contract will first be addressed through mutual discussion.
  • If unresolved, matters will be referred to arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.
  • Jurisdiction for all legal matters will be the courts of Tiruvannamalai, Tamil Nadu.

📞 9. Contact & Communication

  • All formal communications must be in writing (email or signed letter).
  • Site-level instructions given to workers without the contractor's knowledge will not be the contractor's responsibility.
  • The client must designate one point of contact for all project-related decisions.
